The VIT Chess Club

C-Cube (Chess Club) VIT Pune is the ultimate hub for chess enthusiasts on campus. We are dedicated to promoting the game of chess, nurturing strategic thinking, and building a strong community of players, from beginners to grandmasters in the making.

Club Activities

We organize a range of activities to keep the 64 squares exciting, including tournaments, workshops on strategy, and casual play sessions.
