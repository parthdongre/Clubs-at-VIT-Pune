VIT Entrepreneurship Development Cell

The VIT Entrepreneurship Development Cell (VEDC) is a student-run organization dedicated to fostering the spirit of entrepreneurship and innovation on campus. We believe in empowering the next generation of founders, innovators, and business leaders by providing them with the right resources, mentorship, and platform.

What We Offer

Our initiatives include startup bootcamps, pitch competitions, and networking events designed to turn ideas into reality.
