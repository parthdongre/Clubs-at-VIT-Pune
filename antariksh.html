VIT's Astronomy and Space Exploration Club

Antariksh Club is the official astronomy and space exploration club of VIT Pune. We are a community of stargazers, explorers, and science enthusiasts dedicated to unraveling the mysteries of the cosmos. Our mission is to ignite a passion for space science and provide a platform for students to explore their celestial interests.

Our Cosmic Activities

We venture into the universe through stargazing sessions, rocketry workshops, astrophotography contests, and guest lectures from scientists.
