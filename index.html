About    Life at VIT   Events at VIT

Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT) in Pune is well-known for its dynamic and diverse student club culture, which forms a core part of the campus experience. The institute is home to over 40 technical, cultural, and social clubs that provide students with a platform to explore their passions beyond academics. Technical clubs like the Robotics Forum, V-BUG (Linux User Group), and Team Veloce Racing are highly active, participating in national competitions and fostering innovation. On the cultural front, clubs like Antarang manage major events and bring together students interested in music, drama, and dance. These student-run organizations are crucial for developing leadership skills, encouraging teamwork, and allowing students to apply theoretical knowledge to practical, real-world projects.
