International Society of Automation - VIT Pune

The International Society of Automation (ISA) VIT Pune Student Section is a technical club focused on the fields of automation, instrumentation, and control systems. Our goal is to prepare students for careers in the core engineering industry by providing practical knowledge and hands-on experience.

Our Focus Areas

We conduct events and workshops centered around key industrial technologies like PLC, SCADA, Robotics, and more.
